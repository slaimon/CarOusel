a car is described by its frame. It is intended that the wheels of the car are on the plane XZ and its front is toward -Z. A box of the car is also provided.
glm::mat4 frame
local frame of the car. the car front is on -z halfspace

box3 box
box is the bounding box of the car, expressed in "frame" coordinates
